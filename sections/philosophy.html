How I Work

Three principles that guide every project I touch.

  1. 01

    Ship first. Polish with feedback.

    Real users reveal what no spec doc can. I get working software into production quickly, then iterate on what the data and users actually ask for — not what I imagine they'll need.

  2. 02

    Use AI as a force multiplier, not a crutch.

    Claude and Copilot handle scaffolding, brainstorming, and boilerplate. I focus on architecture, product reasoning, and the tradeoffs that actually matter. LLMs don't replace senior judgement — they give you more time to exercise it.

  3. 03

    Code for the engineer who inherits it.

    Readability and intentional structure are not "nice to haves". The code I write today is a debt or an asset for whoever touches it next — including future me at 2am during an incident.
